• Grahak360 Testing: Survey

    Grahak360 is a comprehensive contrived Customer Relationship Management Tool for Predictive Analytics in Supply Chain Management


  • This is the Grahak360 Testing: Survey page.

    You are being asked to contribute 4 minutes of your time to complete the Grahak360 Testing: Survey. This note's objective is to inform you, the survey participant, of your rights as a survey volunteer and the survey's purpose.

     

    What is the purpose of this survey?

    The primary goal of this research is to evaluate the Grahak360 web application.  The purpose of the survey is to examine the demographic study of the users, as well as comprehend website aesthetic preferences, users' overall happiness, relatability, usability, and functioning of Grahak360 from the users' perspective by analyzing the collected data. You will need to click on the check box at the end of this page and submit the consent. You will be automatically directed to the Grahak360 web application link and an online questionnaire. The questionnaire will take you approximately 4 minutes to complete. You will be asked to finish this questionnaire.

     
    What are some of the reasons you would wish to participate in this survey as a volunteer?

    Your participation in the study, on the other hand, will help us better understand users' perspectives on the Grahak360 web application and, if necessary, update the web application with essential improvements. Furthermore, the feedback and implications gained through this survey would be quite beneficial to us.

     

    Is it obligatory for you to participate in this research?

    It is entirely up to you whether or not you participate in this survey. You will not lose any rights, services, or advantages as a result of your withdrawal if you choose not to participate or if you want to participate but subsequently decide you no longer want to. The survey is entirely optional.

     

    What exactly am I supposed to do?

    You've been asked to participate in a survey study on the Grahak360 web application. You must then complete an online survey in the next step. Your involvement is entirely at your discretion.


    What are the procedures you'll be expected to follow?

    In the following, the procedure for completing this questionnaire is being phased out:


    Step 1: Click the download sample data set link to download two data sets, 'Main' and 'Prediction,' for uploading to the Grahak360 online application.

    Step 2: Go to the Grahak 360 interface link and click it.

    (We strongly advise you to go through the User Guide in the About section to get a better understanding of Grahak360 and to make the survey-taking process go more smoothly.)

    Step 3: Utilize the Data Visualization and Analysis tool.

    Step 4: Use RFM and Hybrid segmentation approaches to segment your customers.

    Step 5: Carry out Customer Classification by selecting the best classifier for prediction from a variety of classification models.

    Step 6: Use this interface to perform sales forecasting and get the data in a variety of ways.

    Step 7: Create product suggestion association rules and analyze product recommendation data for decision-making.

    Step 8: Create and evaluate various visualizations connected to forensic features of a dataset, such as the turnover rate.

    Step 9: Use the Customer linked Prediction function to forecast the number of transactions and lifetime value of customers.

    Step 10: Proceed to the survey form to complete the questionnaires and offer your useful recommendations and views.


    The data collected in this survey will be utilized to evaluate and analyze the Grahak360 web app. The data collected will not be used or shared with any other parties. This Jotform application collects information that is absolutely safe.

    Please feel free to contact us with any queries you may have concerning this survey study (teammettleamigos5@gmail.com). Thank you for your willingness to take part in this survey. Without your assistance, we will not be able to achieve our goals.
